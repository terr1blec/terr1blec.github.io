About

I am a MPhil student at the Data Science and Analytics Thrust, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), advised by Prof. Zhijiang Guo.

Prior to this, I obtained a BSc degree in Data Science from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen.

My current research focuses on constructing executable environments and synthesizing tool-use data.
